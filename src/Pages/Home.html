Machine tools

Home to India’s first integrated machine tool park, Karnataka is a major hub for machine tool manufacturing, contributing 52% to the nation’s production.

Electronic design system & manufacturing

Karnataka boasts India's largest chip design hub with over 85 fabless chip design houses. The state contributes 10% to the national electronics output and holds a 40% national share in electronic design.

Auto/EV

Karnataka is the fourth-largest automobile producer in India, contributing 8.5% to the national output. With five auto clusters, seven major OEMs and over 50 component manufacturers, the state also leads in EV innovation, making it a top choice for auto investments.

Aerospace and Defence

Karnataka leads India's Aerospace and Defence industry, producing 67% of the nation's aircraft and helicopter manufacturing for defence services in India.

Textiles and Handloom

Karnataka, the Garment Capital of India, leads in the textile and apparel sector. The state produces 49% of India's silk, 12% of its wool, and 6% of its cotton, making it the top silk producer in the country.

Food and Agro-processing

Karnataka, a key player in India's food processing industry, leads in agriculture by producing 70% of the nation's coffee, 60% of its ragi and 60% of safflower. The state is also known for its diverse milk, meat and fisheries production.
